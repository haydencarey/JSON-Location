Who's Asleep and Who's Awake

Here's an-easy-to-use application that can determine if someone is asleep or awake depending on their location
Simply input the latitude and longitude coordinates of your desired location below.

Tips for formatting your coordinates:

  1. The coordinates should be in this format: 41.40338, 22.17403.
  2. Use the degree symbol as a decimal spot
  3. List your latitude coordinates before the longitude coordinates
  4. A south latitude coordinate is negative. A west longitude coordinate is negative
